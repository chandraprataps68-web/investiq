How to use: Add 5-10 stocks to your watchlist. Click "Take OPEN Snapshot" once at market open (9:30 AM), and "Take CLOSE Snapshot" at market close (3:30 PM). Do this for 15 days. Then download the CSV and analyse which signal combos (scanner + confluence + bias) predicted profitable movement.
Auto-snapshot: When you open this tab during market hours (9:15-10:00 AM or 15:00-15:45 PM), the app auto-captures if you haven't already taken today's snapshot for that session. Manual button always works.
Storage: All data lives in this browser's localStorage. Clearing browser data deletes it. Download CSV regularly to keep a backup.
